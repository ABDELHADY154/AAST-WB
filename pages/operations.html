Why Choose Plastic Clinic?

Every physician at Plastic Clinic is a board-certified plastic surgeon. Why is this important? All physicians across the country must have a basic medical license. Any licensed physician can perform plastic procedures without specializing in plastic surgery. Unfortunately, many doctors simply attend a weekend course for a plastic procedure, advertise themselves as plastic surgeons, and deliver results that simply don’t provide the same levels of safety and quality as those provided by board-certified plastic surgeons.

Body Procedures

Even a healthy diet and rigorous fitness regimen can fail to produce the toned, chiseled look you’ve worked so hard for. Fortunately, our board-certified plastic surgeons offer a full suite of body contouring procedures to help you achieve the sculpted shape you deserve

Facial Surgery

Your face is the first thing people notice about you. As time passes, the aging process causes facial tissue to wrinkle, sag and lose youthful fullness. While there is no way to stop the hands of time, facial plastic surgery can dramatically improve the way you look and feel about yourself.
